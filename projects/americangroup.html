American Group

A collective of Commercial Real Estate firms backed by over a century of investment experience that together acquire, develop and manage multi-family housing income properties.

I worked in-house to lead digital strategy and development as part of their initiative to implement state-of-the-art investment vehicles that leverage Opportunity Zone legislation to provide tax efficient, high yield returns while simultaneously benefiting underserved communities.

Market Research

Identified key themes in competition and focused business goals on customer needs. Discovered industry standards and opportunities for great web design and marketing strategy.

Branding

Built a Style Guide that included font pairings, color palette & rules, custom logo design, recurring design elements, and best practices for photography selection and editing.

Web Design

Designed website using a component-based structure according to atomic design principles. Started with wireframes to determine site layout and user flows. Subsequently built mockups using Sketch and Illustrator.

Development

Instantiated an AWS instance and LEMP stack. Build custom, responsive Wordpress theme to house design using Underscores and Bootstrap. Custom configured AJAX login, transitions, animations, and other complex elements with Javascript, jQuery, PHP, MySQL.

Content & Copywriting

Wrote and edited all investment, branding, and education materials for website and marketing. Built custom interactive content, such as tax savings calculator and an explainer video animation.

Marketing & Investor Relations

Was eventually promoted to Director position and headed all marketing initiatives.
