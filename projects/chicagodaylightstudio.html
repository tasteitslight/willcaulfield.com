Chicago Daylight Studio

A state-of-the-art photo, video, and events studio located in the West Loop, offering production facilities and services for commercial and editorial photography, videography, and corporate off-sites.

In partnership with a local photographer, I executed the vision, marketing, business developement, and entrepreneurship required to established CDS as a premier location for Chicago's international photographic productions.
