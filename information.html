William Caulfield

will@caulfield.co

Santa Monica, CA

After a 2+ year stint in Chicago co-founding a commercial photography studio and helping a number of companies with marketing strategy and digital design, I've recently moved to Santa Monica in search of new challenges and opportunities.

I have worked on great projects in finance, real estate, production, and healthcare; and in various roles from design and development to strategy, marketing, publishing, and branding.

My intent is to ground products in evidence-based, user centered principles – simultaneously keeping people at the heart of the process, while meeting clients’ organizational needs.

I am interested in systems-level-change and how it is catalyzed by digital tools to create tangible effects. I am an advocate of inclusive, accessible management, always aiming to provide the best possible experience for everyone. I love all things innovative, healthful and aware.

If you have an interesting project in the works and are in need of a versatile designer, developer, or strategist, I'd be excited to hear all about it. Please reach out and say 'Hello'!
